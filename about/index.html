A little about me...

I am a first year PhD candidate at the Georgia Institute for Technology, where I hope to explore research topics surrounding Numerical Analysis and Computational Science. I appreciate the concept of designing computer simulations to study the world around us and look forward to contributing to the field in due time. Before coming to Georgia Tech, I was engaged in several research activities at the University of Georgia. First and foremost, I was an undergraduate researcher for Professor Henry F. Schaefer at the Center for Computational Quantum Chemistry, where I found my initial passion for computational science. After that, I divied up my time between the Small Satellite Research Laboratory, where I was a part of the SPOC mission's research team and working with Jason Cantarella in the mathematics department.
